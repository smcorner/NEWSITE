License Policy

Effective Date: April 1, 2025 | Infitax Solution, Gandhidham, Gujarat, India

1. License Types

  • Silver Plan (₹150 incl. GST): 1 user, valid for 1 year from activation date.
  • Gold Plan (₹350 incl. GST): Up to 5 users, valid for 1 year from activation date.
  • Platinum Plan (₹5,000 incl. GST): Up to 3 users, valid for lifetime with all future updates.

2. Activation

Each license is activated by entering the registered email and license key in the GSTSyncPro extension. License keys are delivered to your registered email within a few minutes of payment confirmation.

3. Trial Mode

Without a license, GSTSyncPro runs in Trial Mode: all features are visible and functional, but file uploads are disabled. Pre-loaded sample data can be used to explore all features before purchase.

4. Discount Conditions

The 50% promotional launch discount is applied to all purchases across all payment methods including UPI, Credit Card, Debit Card, Net Banking, and Mobile Wallets. All prices displayed on the website are inclusive of GST and reflect the discounted rate. Infitax Solution reserves the right to modify or withdraw this discount at any time without prior notice.

5. License Transfers

Licenses are non-transferable to a different registered email address unless approved by Infitax Solution support on a case-by-case basis.

6. Renewal

Annual plans (Silver, Gold) must be manually renewed before expiry to maintain file upload access. A reminder email will be sent 15 days before expiry.

7. Violation

Sharing license keys beyond the licensed user count will result in immediate license revocation without refund.

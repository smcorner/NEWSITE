Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 1, 2025 | Infitax Solution, Gandhidham, Gujarat, India

1. Introduction

Infitax Solution ("we", "our", "us") operates GSTSyncPro, a Chrome browser extension. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our extension and website.

2. Information We Collect

  • License Activation Data: When you activate a license, we collect your email address and license key solely for the purpose of verifying your license entitlement.
  • Contact Form Data: If you contact us through our website, we collect your name, email, and message content.
  • No Financial Data: GSTSyncPro processes all GST files locally within your browser. This data is never transmitted to, stored on, or accessed by our servers.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To verify and manage your license subscription
  • To send your license key after purchase via email
  • To respond to support and contact inquiries
  • To send important product updates (you may opt out at any time)

4. Data Storage & Security

Your email and license key are transmitted over HTTPS and stored securely. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third party except as required by law.

5. Cookies

Our website may use basic cookies for functionality. No tracking or advertising cookies are used. The extension itself does not use cookies.

6. Children’s Privacy

GSTSyncPro is intended for business and professional use only. We do not knowingly collect information from individuals under 18 years of age.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Continued use of GSTSyncPro after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact

For privacy-related queries: Infitaxsolution@gmail.com | Infitax Solution, Gandhidham, Gujarat, India

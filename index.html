How GSTSyncPro Works

From file upload to ITC summary in four easy steps. No data leaves your browser.

1

Upload 4 Files

Drag & drop your GSTR-2B Purchase, Books Purchase, GSTR-2B CN/DN, and Books CN/DN files directly into the extension.

2

Auto-Reconcile

GSTSyncPro runs 3-pass purchase matching (Exact → Fuzzy → Amount) and 6-pass CN/DN matching automatically.

3

Review & Act

Accept, unmatch, pair, force-pair or mark as "Not Matched" on each record. Use bulk actions for speed.

4

Export Results

Export your reconciliation as Excel (formatted), JSON, or copy the ITC summary directly — Net Claimable ready.

Frequently Asked Questions

Got Questions? We Have Answers.

Everything you need to know about GSTSyncPro, licensing, data privacy, and support.

What is the difference between the plans? ▼
Silver is for 1 user for 1 year (₹150 incl. GST). Gold is for up to 5 users for 1 year (₹350 incl. GST). Platinum is for up to 3 users with lifetime access and all future updates (₹5,000 incl. GST). All plans unlock the same features — only user count and validity differ. All prices shown are after the 50% launch discount.
How do I activate my license? ▼
After purchase, you will receive your license key via email at the address you provide during checkout. Open the GSTSyncPro extension, go to the Activate License screen, enter your email and license key, and click Verify & Activate.
Is my data safe? Does it upload anywhere? ▼
Your financial data never leaves your device. GSTSyncPro processes all files locally inside your browser. Only license validation communicates with our server — no GST data, GSTIN, or invoice data is ever transmitted.
Is the 50% discount available on all payment methods? ▼
Yes — the 50% launch discount applies to all payment methods: UPI, Credit Card, Debit Card, Net Banking, Wallets, and more. All prices shown on the site are already after the discount and inclusive of GST. No coupon code needed.
What file formats are supported for upload? ▼
GSTSyncPro supports Excel (.xlsx, .xls) and CSV formats for all four file types — GSTR-2B Purchase, Books Purchase, GSTR-2B CN/DN, and Books CN/DN.
Can I get a refund? ▼
Refunds are only considered for proven technical deficiencies or service failures caused by Infitax Solution that cannot be resolved within 7 working days. Refunds are not available for change of mind, user-side compatibility issues, or general dissatisfaction. Annual plan requests must be raised within 7 days of purchase. See our full Refund Policy or email Infitaxsolution@gmail.com.
Does GSTSyncPro work offline? ▼
Yes — reconciliation, matching, and export all work offline. An internet connection is only needed during initial license activation and periodic validation checks.
How long does reconciliation take? ▼
For most datasets (up to a few thousand records), GSTSyncPro completes all matching passes in under 10 seconds. Performance depends on your computer and browser, but it is significantly faster than manual spreadsheet reconciliation.
Can I use the extension on multiple computers? ▼
Each license is tied to the number of users — Silver (1 user), Gold (up to 5 users), Platinum (up to 3 users). You can install the extension on multiple devices, but only the licensed number of concurrent users may use file upload features.
